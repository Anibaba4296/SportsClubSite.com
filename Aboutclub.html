About club

Founder of club

This club was founded by a great sportsman named Dheswani Bharti. He has build this kingdom to make players ready to compete at any level.
He says, "Practise like devil and play like angel".

Achivments

Many atheletes from this club have made their way to national team and lot of atheletes are shining like a bright star in many events organised at national level. Our U-17 football has qualified of AFC U-17 Championship held in Dubai. Our volleball team is now one of the best team in India at this moment. Recently one of our athelete has broken record of 200m at national games held in Delhi in january 2020. Atheletes from our club get chance to participate in Khelo India Games. Many atheletes from our club have participated at international level and have won events.


Facilites

We have lush green campus of 900 acres of land designed with all the facilities need by atheletes. We have both indoor and outdoor stadiums in our campus. Our indoor stadiums consists of badminton courts,Volleyball courts and a olympic size swimming pool helping our atheletes to admire new skills to achieve success toward their goals. Our outdoor stadium consists of a football ground along with tracks at outer part. Our campus have well developed medical facilities with world class doctors to ensure fitness of atheletes.
